# About

The Prompt Engineering Guide is a project by [B.Pranay Kumar](https://github.com/Pranay-Kumar-Yadav). It aims to educate researchers and practitioners about prompt engineering, context engineering, RAG, and AI Agents.

B.Pranay Kumar aims to democratize AI research, education, and technologies. Our mission is to enable the next-generation of AI innovators and creators.
